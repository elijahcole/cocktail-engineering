# Richmond Gimlet 

## Ingredients

* 2 oz gin
* 0.75 oz 2:1 simple syrup
* 0.75 oz lime juice
* large sprig of mint

## Instructions

Combine ingredients, shake with ice, fine strain (to remove mint leaf fragments), and serve up.
