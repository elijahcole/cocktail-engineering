# Ingredients

* 8 oz. gin
* 2 oz. blanc vermouth
* 1 oz. water
* 1 tsp. Champagne vinegar
* 4 dashes orange bitters

# Instructions

To prepare:
1. Combine all ingredients in a bottle.
2. Chill in the freezer.

To serve:
1. Pour into chilled glass.
2. Garnish with lemon peel.
